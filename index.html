Gender Equality at Binary Edge Tech

Our Commitment

At Binary Edge Tech, we believe that technology can only reach its fullest potential when it is built by diverse teams. Gender equality is not just a value we uphold — it is a strategic driver of innovation, problem-solving, and long-term sustainability.

Challenges in the IT Sector

  • Low representation of women in leadership and core technical roles.
  • Persistent gender pay gaps across the global IT industry.
  • High attrition rates for women in mid-career stages due to lack of support.
  • Unconscious bias that limits equal growth opportunities.

What Binary Edge Tech is Doing

  • Diverse Hiring Practices → Ensuring balanced representation at all levels.
  • Pay Transparency → Conducting regular salary audits to remove wage gaps.
  • Women in Leadership → Dedicated mentorship and sponsorship programs to build leaders of tomorrow.
  • Returnship Programs → Re-integrating women professionals after career breaks.
  • STEM Outreach → Supporting scholarships and training programs for women in AI, cloud, and cybersecurity.

Our Impact (2023–2025)

  • Increased women in our workforce from 29% → 37%.
  • Boosted women in leadership positions from 16% → 24%.
  • Reduced our gender pay gap from 3.2% → 0.5%.

These numbers reflect just the beginning of our journey. By 2030, Binary Edge Tech aims to achieve full gender parity in leadership roles and serve as a benchmark for equality in the IT industry.

Gender Equality Roadmap at Binary Edge Tech

Year 1: Building Foundations

  • Conduct company-wide gender pay audits.
  • Launch inclusive hiring practices for all departments.
  • Introduce unconscious bias & inclusivity training.

Year 2: Driving Growth

  • Expand women-in-leadership mentorship programs.
  • Launch returnship programs for women rejoining the workforce.
  • Provide scholarships & upskilling programs in AI, Cloud, and Cybersecurity.

Year 3: Achieving Impact

  • Reach 40% women workforce representation.
  • Achieve 30% women in leadership positions.
  • Maintain a zero gender pay gap policy.
  • Establish Binary Edge Tech as an industry role model for inclusivity.
