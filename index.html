Gender Equality

Role Model: Sheryl Sandberg (Former COO, Facebook | Founder of LeanIn.Org)

Advocate for women’s leadership in technology, equal pay, and workplace reforms. Author of “Lean In.”

SDG 5 Relevance: Women make up only 26–28% of the tech workforce globally. We aim to fix gaps in leadership, pay equity, and representation.
